i'm stoked by the waybackmachine

Man, I was just thinking the other day about all the content I’ve lost over the years switching blogging platforms. Then I listened to a podcast (most likely from Leo Laporte’s TWIT network) and found out about the WayBackMachine on archive.org… I know I’ve heard of it before but always thought “what is that good for anyway???”

Well today I checked it out, and all my blog posts from 2004 and 2005 are on there 😃
There are tons of other sites I’ve lost, most of which I probably won’t ever find not because the way back machine doesn’t have them, but because I can’t remember the URLs…

Anyways, it was just fun reading some of the stuff I wrote a few years ago. I actually sounded smarter back then 😛
