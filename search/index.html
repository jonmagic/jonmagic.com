How to search

This semantic search understands the meaning behind your queries, not just keywords. Try searching for:

  • Topics: "software engineering", "work-life balance", "technology"
  • Concepts: "learning new skills", "career growth", "project management"
  • Questions: "how to set up development environment", "advice for new developers"

The search will find posts that are conceptually related to your query, even if they don't contain the exact words you searched for.
